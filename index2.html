Prospective Students: Important Information Before Contacting Me

If you are considering working with me as your supervisor, please read the following guidelines carefully. These points outline my expectations and the preparation required before we arrange a meeting or begin supervision.

Develop your own research proposal

I encourage students to formulate their own research ideas. Once you have write a tentative research proposal, I will provide feedback, guidance, and theoretical grounding.

Prepare a structured research proposal

Your initial proposal should include research title, research questions, literature review, and methodology. This does not need to be perfect, but it should demonstrate that you have thought carefully about your topic.

Evaluate your timeline

Please assess the time you will need to complete your proposal and ensure that your schedule aligns with your relevant application deadlines. This will help maintain a smooth review process.

Read one of my papers

To understand my research orientation, writing style, and theoretical approach, please read at least one of my published papers before contacting me. This allows us to have a more productive conversation and ensures that your proposed topic fits within my areas of expertise.

預計申請碩博士

如果你正在考慮找我指導研究，請先閱讀以下說明。這些內容將幫助你了解我的期待，也能讓我們之後的討論更有效率。

請先自行發展研究構想

我鼓勵學生主動提出自己的研究點子，而不是等待我提供題目。當你完成一份初步的研究計畫後，我會再協助你進一步細化問題、補充理論與方法上的建議。

準備一份基本且有架構的研究計畫

你的初步研究計畫建議包含以下內容：研究題目、研究問題、相關文獻與背景、研究方法。計畫不需非常完整，但應能呈現你已認真思考過自己的研究主題。

請先確認你的時間安排

請評估你需要多少時間來完成研究計畫，並檢查是否能配合相關申請或系所的截止日期。良好的時間規劃有助於後續的討論與審閱。

請先閱讀我至少一篇論文

為了讓你更了解我的研究方向、寫作方式與理論取向，建議在與我聯繫前先讀一篇我已發表的研究。這能讓我們的對話更有焦點，也能幫助你判斷自己的研究是否與我的專長相符。
